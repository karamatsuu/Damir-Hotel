Blind spots the council caught (peer review only)
⚠ Guest confirmation missing (flagged by 3 of 5 reviewers)

After submitting the inquiry form, guests receive no acknowledgment — no on-page success state, no copy to their inbox. International travelers booking from abroad get radio silence and assume the hotel is closed or the form failed. Fix: add a guest confirmation email to form-endpoint.gs and verify the client-side success/error UI. Do this at the same time as deploying the endpoint.

⚠ i18n gap — file-evidenced (flagged by 2 of 5 reviewers)

i18n.js is listed as a modified file on this branch. The site has internationalization scaffolding, but no advisor verified it's wired up, complete, or working. Target guests include Europeans and Japanese cultural tourists. A form that works but communicates in one language is still a conversion problem. Audit i18n.js before launch.

The First Principles Thinker — 3-step booking breakdown
A booking requires 3 steps: (1) guest inquires, (2) inquiry reaches owner, (3) owner responds. Step 2 is broken. Guest questions: "Can I trust this price?" (UZS fails), "Is this place real?" (no Est. year), "Can I actually book?" (comparison-shopping on Booking.com — need response-time SLA).
