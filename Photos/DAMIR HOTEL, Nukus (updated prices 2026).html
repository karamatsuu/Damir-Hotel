About this property *Comfortable Accommodations:* DAMIR HOTEL in Nukus offers family rooms with air-conditioning, private bathrooms, and city views. Each room includes a tea and coffee maker, walk-in shower, and free toiletries. *Essential Facilities:* Guests enjoy a terrace, free WiFi, and free on-site private parking. Additional amenities include a lounge, shuttle service, 24-hour front desk, and daily housekeeping. *Dining Experience:* A buffet breakfast is served daily, complemented by a restaurant offering diverse dishes. The on-site minimarket provides convenience for all visitors. *Local Attractions:* Located 3 km from Nukus Airport, the hotel is near an ice-skating rink and boating opportunities. Guests appreciate the restaurant, room cleanliness, and attentive staff. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it *9.3* for a two- person trip.
