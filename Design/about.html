Who we are

A modern house behind a classic façade.

Damir Hotel was built on a simple belief: that real luxury is quiet. Behind a stately white exterior, we made interiors that feel calm and contemporary — warm oak joinery, honed stone, soft light and space used generously.

From a 24-hour welcome to a generous breakfast buffet, everything here is arranged to make a stay feel effortless. Guests regularly mention the central location, spotless rooms, helpful reception team and comfortable beds.
