Who we are

A contemporary landmark of Nukus hospitality.

Damir Hotel was built on a bold vision: that true luxury should feel both grand and genuinely welcoming. Crystal chandeliers, marble surfaces and considered modern interiors create a setting that impresses without feeling distant.

From a 24-hour welcome to a generous breakfast buffet, bar, fitness room and billiards lounge, everything here is arranged to make every stay feel exceptional. Guests consistently mention the prime location, spotless rooms, friendly staff and the quality of the spaces.
